With the promise to innovate, once again, in terms of visual and special effects, the film will follow the story of Jake Sully (Sam Worthington), 13 years after the events of the first film. Now married to Neytiri (Zoë Saldaña) and father of five children, the protagonist and his family must run away from home and explore the regions of Pandora in search of refuge with the oceanic Metkayina clan.
